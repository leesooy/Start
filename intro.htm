I was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea. My world didn’t open yet until I got to know about Distance Learning.

Distance Learning

It was a turning point of my life when I started learning throughout Internet. My inflexible schedule with two young babies and tight budget were always my cliche excuses to put myself off from learning at school.

I realized that a learning process could be much easier and available to than I ever thought when I was introduced to Massive Open Online Learning Courses(MOOC),which offer large-scale interactive participation and open access through the World Wide Web or other network technologies.

Distance Learning opens the way to see the word for me. And now, I hope I can inspire people who are eager to learn just like I was.
